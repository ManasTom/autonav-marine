Get To Know Us

Delivering Excellence in Marine Solutions

At AUTONAV MARINE FZE, we take pride in being a trusted leader in marine automation, navigation, and integrated control systems for over two decades. Since our inception in 2001, we have been dedicated to providing innovative, reliable, and cost-effective automation solutions that help vessels operate with precision, efficiency, and complete compliance with global maritime standards. Our expertise extends across automation system design, installation, retrofitting, calibration, and maintenance — empowering shipowners and operators with smarter, safer, and more connected vessel operations

Driven by our values of quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, AUTONAV MARINE FZE continues to be the preferred partner for marine automation solutions across the Middle East, Africa, and South Asia.
